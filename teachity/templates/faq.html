Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the video/audio in my classroom working?

  • Make sure you're using Google Chrome. WebRTC, the video chat interface Teachity uses, only works on Google Chrome at the moment. Other browsers should be adding support soon
  • Check here to install the latest version of Google Chrome
  • Close any other open applications that may be slowing your computer down
  • If it still isn't working, message your tutor and schedule an appointment via Skype

Can I use Teachity on my phone or mobile device?

It depends on the device. We're working hard to add official support for the iPhone, iPad, and Android devices so stay tuned!
